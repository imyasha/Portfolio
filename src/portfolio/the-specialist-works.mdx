In late 2017 I was approached by one of the UK's leading media agencies,
The Specialist Works, to redesign and develop their new corporate website,
combining 5 of their international websites into one. Work commenced in
December 2017 and by April 2018 we finally launched the new website, based
on WordPress and a more stable infrastructure.
